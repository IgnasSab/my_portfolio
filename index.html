About Me

Hey, good to see you here!

My name is Ignas Šablinskas. I was born in Vilnius, Lithuania and currently studying in the Netherlands, Technology University of Eindhoven and looking for Summer internship opportunities in Software Development with no hesitation of moving abroad.

Double Bachelor Student of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science

Passionate about Competitive Programming and Problem Solving

I embrace challenges and continuous learning with a steep learning curve.
