About Me

My names is Ignas Šablinskas and I am from Vilnius, Lithuania. Throughout highschool I started to focus on Mathematics and Computer Science. I was learning my first programming languages and doing my first baby projects with them (see simulations, console) and naturally shifted to larger ones later. For mathematics I constantly studied extra curriculum lot which lead me to participate in olympiads and even represent my capital city. Later on I graduated highschool with honors and and continued my studies in Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e) as a Double Bachelor of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science. I am currently in the second year and have a GPA of 8.9 in both of the aforementioned fields, moreover, I got accepted to Honors Academy of Competitive Programming and Problem Solving which I enjoy quite a lot. Overall, I am a determined and ambitious individual, committed to excelling in my studies. I set very high standards for myself in every endeavor and continuously strive for improvement. My discipline and determination ensures that I can embrace and commit to new challenges easily. I am eager to contribute my skills and learn from diverse experiences to grow both personally and professionally.
